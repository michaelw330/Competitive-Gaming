    I’ve always loved video games, and especially competitive gaming is one of my biggest passions. One of the unique things about competitive gaming compared to sports is that there aren’t any physical limitations. No one is born with certain advantages like being extremely tall in a game like basketball. While I love sports too, this is one of the most interesting things about competitive gaming, is that truly anyone can be the best.

    However, some games require intense mechanical abilities as well, but this varies from game to game, some take an incredible amount of mechanical ability and coordination, and others take literally none. Because of this, each game is its own art form, because they require such different skills. Despite these differences, gaming is really about how you exercise your mind within the game, similarly to chess.
